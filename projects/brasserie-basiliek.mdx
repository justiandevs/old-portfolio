## What is Brasserie Basiliek?
Brasserie Basiliek is a small Basiliek (place to eat and drink) in the city of Venlo, in the Netherlands.

## The build process
I built this website to help Aphelos, the owner of this company was very busy with other projects and so asked if I could take on this project. The restaurant wanted a modern clean looking website and the client wanted this to be made with Elementor (a Wordpress page builder).
